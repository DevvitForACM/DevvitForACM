Audio Files Location
====================

Place the following audio files in this directory:

BGM (Background Music):
- bgmdummy.mp3 (will be replaced with your BGM)

Sound Effects:
- coin1.mp3 (first coin collecting sound)
- coin2.mp3 (second coin collecting sound)
- death1.mp3 (first death sound)
- death2.mp3 (second death sound)
- jump1.mp3 (first jump sound)
- jump2.mp3 (second jump sound)
- jump3.mp3 (third jump sound)

These files will be automatically loaded by the settings service.
